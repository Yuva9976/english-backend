## üí° Tips for Better Listening

1. **Listen multiple times** - Don't worry if you don't understand everything the first time
2. **Focus on key words** - You don't need to understand every word
3. **Use context clues** - The situation helps you understand the meaning
4. **Take notes** - Write down key information as you listen
5. **Practice regularly** - Listen to English every day for 10-15 minutes
