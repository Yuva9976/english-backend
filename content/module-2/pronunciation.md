## Sentence Stress and Rhythm

In English, we stress **content words** (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs) and unstress **function words** (articles, prepositions, pronouns).

**Example:**
- **I WANT to GO to the STORE.**
  - Stressed: WANT, GO, STORE
  - Unstressed: I, to, to, the

## Tips for Better Pronunciation

1. **Listen actively** - Watch English movies and TV shows
2. **Record yourself** - Compare your pronunciation with native speakers
3. **Practice daily** - Even 5 minutes a day makes a difference
4. **Use a mirror** - Watch your mouth movements
5. **Don't be afraid** - Making mistakes is part of learning!
